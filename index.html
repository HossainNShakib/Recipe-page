Simple Omelet Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats.

Preparation time

  • Total: Appromximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 05 minutes
  • Cooking: 05 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optional fillings: cheese, diced vegetables, coocekd meats, herbs.

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs: In a bowl, beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until they are well mixed. You can add a tablespoon of water or milk for a fluffier texture
  2. Heat the pan: Place a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and add butter or oil
  3. Cook the Omelet:Once the butter is melted and bubbling, pour in the eggs. Tilt the pan to ensure the eggs evenly coat the suftace.
  4. Add fillings (Optional): When the eggs begin to set at the edges but are still slightly runny in the middle, spinkle your chosen fillings over ane half of the Omelet.
  5. Fold and serve: As the Omelet continuse to cook, carefully lift one edge and folt it over the fillings. Let it cook for another minute, then slide it onto a plate.
  6. Enjoy: Serve hot, with additional salt and pepper if needed.
